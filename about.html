About

Motivations

Why did I create this application? I want to improve upon our team communication through an open platform available 24/7.

This platform gives us the opportunity to acknowledge issues that otherwise may have gone un-noticed. Every piece of information received will be reviewed and disscussed to find the best solution. To begin feedback navigate to the "Submission" tab.

The positives!

Just as important as resolving issues and identifying store struggles is recognizing the potential to build upon culture. I hope to encourage everyone to leave feedback on what you'd like to see added, removed and/or changed from this website to make it the best experience possible. Rolling with these positive vibes, I would like to see ideas for team activities such as trips, cook-offs, game nights, etc...
